## Mashed Cauliflower

Love the taste of mashed potatoes but looking for a lower carb version? Try mashed cauliflower. This recipe will yield a smooth, creamy side dish that's silky smooth and has 75% fewer carbs that [mashed potatoes](https://www.makebetterfood.com/recipes/mashed-potatoes/). It was a huge hit at our Thanksgiving dinner last year thanks to the slightly earthy aroma and velvety texture.

[![Mashed Cauliflower](https://www.makebetterfood.com/recipes/mashed-cauliflower/mashed-cauliflower.webp)](https://www.makebetterfood.com/recipes/mashed-cauliflower/mashed-cauliflower_large.webp)

**Nutritional Info**

Carbs per serving: 8g

- 4 quarts water
- Bring water to a boil in a large stockpot.
- 1080g / 1 head cauliflower
- Wash and cut cauliflower into small pieces. Cook in boiling water until soft, about 8 to 10 minutes.
- Drain and place in a salad spinner or between paper towels and pat to dry. Place in a food processor.
- ¼ cup Parmesan cheese
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 tablespoon cream cheese, softened
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon pepper
- Add remaining ingredients to food processor and process until smooth.
- Transfer to a bowl and serve.
