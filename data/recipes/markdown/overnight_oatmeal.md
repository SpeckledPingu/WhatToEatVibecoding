## Overnight Oatmeal

This oatmeal parfait requires no cooking. Just combine ingredients in a mason jar, put it in the fridge overnight, and enjoy in the morning.

[![Overnight Oatmeal](https://www.makebetterfood.com/recipes/overnight-oatmeal/overnight-oatmeal.webp)](https://www.makebetterfood.com/recipes/overnight-oatmeal/overnight-oatmeal_large.webp)

**Nutritional Info**

T1D Carbs-per-gram ratio: **0.1541**

Serving Size: 178g (about 28g carbs)

- 71g / ⅔ cup old-fashioned rolled oats
- 136g / ⅔ cup Greek yogurt
- ⅔ cup water
- 1 teaspoon vanilla or almond extract
- Combine ingredients in a large mason jar (or divide between two mason jars). Cover tightly with lid and shake. Set in fridge overnight to chill.
- Remove from fridge next morning and enjoy!
